Technology & Dance

Technology and dance have always worked hand-in-hand to create new and exciting ways of expressing art. Over time, technology has helped shape how dance is created, performed, and experienced by audiences. Technology has opened up many new possibilities for dancers and choreographers.

What is Technology?

Technology refers to the tools and systems people create to make life easier, solve problems, or improve things. It can include anything from the simplest tools, like pencils, to the most advanced inventions, like computers and smartphones.

How do we see Technology in Dance?

In the world of dance, technology includes things like lighting, sound systems, digital video equipment, and even computer software. These tools have allowed dancers and choreographers to experiment with new ways of creating and performing dance. Technology also helps to preserve dance performances, making them available to wider audiences all over the world.
